What is it?

Rechargeable card for Metro, Metrobus, and Trolebus. Works on all lines. No need for cash tickets.

Where to buy:

  • Any Metro station ticket booth
  • Metrobus kiosks (blue stands)

How to recharge:

  • At any Metro ticket booth (cash only)
  • Metrobus machines (cash or card)
  • OXXO stores (say "quiero recargar mi Tarjeta CDMX")

Minimum recharge: $10 MXN

Fares:

  • Metro: $5 MXN per ride
  • Metrobus: $6 MXN per ride
